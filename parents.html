Important Information!


There are a number of things you can do to help protect your kids online as well as
make them aware of the dangers of the internet.

See the slideshow below for more!


Make it a family affair


The most important thing to maintain safe Internet use within your household is ‘open communication’.
Talk to your children regularly about their online activities.


Create online time limits


While the Internet is a fun way to communicate, children sometimes need to be reminded that real-life communication is just as important.


Talk to your children


Your children are likely to have far greater knowledge than you about the internet so speak to them about where they go, what sites they visit.


Have a family email address


Set up a family email account which can be used specifically to
register for websites, competitions etc.
