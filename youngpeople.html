Tip 1:

Tip 2:

Important Information


Here at Digital Safe, we know how important it is to get the right information.
So, we've picked some of the top apps and given some information related to them.
You can find these below!

Applications:

Instagram

Instagram is a picture and video sharing app. Users can post content on their profile grid or to their stories, which last 24 hours. You can follow your friends, family, celebrities and companies on Instagram.

Facebook

Facebook is a social media site which lets you connect with others and share things like comments, photos and videos through your own profile page. You can add friends, write on people’s pages and posts.

Snapchat

The Snapchat app lets you send photos, short videos or messages to your friends. Pictures/videos, known as 'Snaps', appear temporarily before disappearing, though they can be captured via screenshots.

Tik Tok

TikTok is a social media platform that lets you create, share and discover 60 second videos. You can use music and effects to enhance your videos and you can also browse others' videos.
